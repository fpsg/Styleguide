foodpanda Design System

The foodpanda Design System is an attempt to reduce complexity, increase quality and align our front-end design with our mobile apps. Our attempt to do this is through a design system, that will simplify building digital products by standardizing and unifying the design language we use.

For feedback and questions regarding the Design System, feel free to reach out to design@foodpanda.com
